About

My Story

Hi I'm sage, a UX/UI designer skilled at empathizing with people to create user centered design solutions. Through my studies in biology I developed skills in quantitative and qualitative research that can help me investigate user pain points to create design solutions for better user interaction. My work in emergency animal medicine has helped me learn how to understand and empathize with clients and how to quickly come up with creative solutions. I am motivated to use my curiosity and creative skills to solve problems and design satisfying product experiences. I grew up in the mountains of Asheville, NC and I love spending time outdoors hiking, biking, and doing yoga. I am an animal lover, and I've spent much of my time volunteering with animals all around the world.
