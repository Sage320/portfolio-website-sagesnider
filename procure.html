Reflection

What went well:

This was my last project in my Career Foundry course, and based on feedback I've gotten I think it's my best piece I've designed so far! This project was about how to create a cohesive brand, and incorporate the brand's values and principles into the design. I think this project helped me learn how to create a cohesive project from start to finish.

What could be improved:

I think some of the high fidelity screens could be improved, because they have too much information, look busy, and could have their spacing improved. I could also develop the desktop screens further, by designing additional pages to illustrate other functions of the app.
